    Ride Saturday

    £425

    The Ride Saturday is a versatile all-mountain freestyle snowboard designed for riders who want to dominate the park while still carving confidently across the resort. Featuring a Twin Hybrid Camber profile, it combines camber between the bindings for powerful edge control and stability with rocker at the tip and tail for a playful, catch-free feel. The Performance™ Core, crafted from a mix of aspen, bamboo, and paulownia, ensures durability and responsiveness, while Carbon Array 3™ enhances pop and control for high-speed turns and landings. The Slimewalls® technology absorbs impacts and vibrations, delivering a smooth and durable ride. The board's clean, modern top sheet with a retro-inspired graphic pairs perfectly with the bold, colorful base, making it stand out on the slopes. Whether you're hitting jumps, locking into rails, or cruising the mountain, the Ride Saturday is built for endless fun and progression.
