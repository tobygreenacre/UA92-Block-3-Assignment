    Salomon Lotus

    £310

    The Salomon Lotus is a confidence-boosting, all-mountain snowboard designed for beginner to intermediate riders looking for an easy, smooth, and forgiving ride. Featuring a Flat Out Camber profile, it provides a stable and predictable feel with just the right amount of flex for effortless turns and progression. The Directional Twin Shape allows for versatility, making it great for both regular and switch riding, while the Aspen Core keeps it lightweight and durable. The BiteFree Edges ensure a catch-free experience, making it ideal for learning new skills and gaining confidence across all terrain. The soft white-to-lavender gradient top sheet with futuristic geometric accents gives the board a sleek, modern look, while the Salomon branding on the base adds a stylish touch. Whether cruising groomers, exploring the resort, or getting into freestyle, the Lotus is the perfect board for riders who want to progress with ease and have fun on every run.
