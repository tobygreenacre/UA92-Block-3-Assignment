    Returns & Exchange Policy

    At Poudre, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you need to return or exchange an item, please review our policy below.

    Returns & Exchanges:

    • Items may be returned or exchanged within 30 days of purchase with proof of purchase.
    • Items must be unused, unworn, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.
    • Snowboards must be unridden and in brand-new condition.
    • Used or damaged items are not eligible for return or exchange.

    Return Process:

    • Contact us at Returns@poudre.com to initiate a return.
    • Ship the item back to us. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.
    • Once we receive and inspect the item, we will process a refund or exchange within 5-7 business days.

    Non-Returnable Items:

    • Clearance or sale items (final sale)
    • Custom or special-order items
    • Gift cards
    • Items that show signs of use or damage

    Defective or Incorrect Items:

    • If you receive a defective or incorrect item, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order. We will provide a prepaid return label and arrange for a replacement or full refund.

    Refunds:

    • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
    • Shipping fees are non-refundable.
    • If returning an item purchased with a gift card, store credit will be issued.

    For any questions, feel free to reach out to us at Returns@poudre.com.
