    FAQs

    What types of snowboards do you sell?

    • We specialize in high-quality snowboards for all skill levels, including freestyle, all-mountain, and powder boards.

    Are your snowboards suitable for beginners?

    • Absolutely! We offer beginner-friendly boards that provide stability and easy maneuverability for those new to snowboarding.

    Do you sell bindings and boots?

    • No, we specialize exclusively in snowboards. However, we provide recommendations for compatible bindings and boots.

    How often do you restock sold-out boards?

    • Restocks vary by brand and model. Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on new arrivals.

    Do you offer custom snowboards?

    • At this time, we do not offer custom snowboards, but we carry a variety of designs to suit different styles and preferences.

    Can I get advice on choosing the right snowboard?

    • Yes! Email us at help@poudre.com, and our team will be happy to help you find the perfect snowboard for your riding style.
