    Our Story

    Founded in 2024 by Toby Greenacre, Poudre was created with one goal in mind—to bring the best quality snowboards to riders of all levels. As a small business, we take pride in carefully selecting each board we sell, ensuring they meet our high standards for performance, durability, and style. Every board in our collection is handpicked based on what we believe to be the best and coolest options available. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, we’re here to help you find the perfect board for your next adventure. Thank you for supporting our passion for snowboarding!
