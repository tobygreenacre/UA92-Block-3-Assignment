    Burton Story Board Camber

    £585

    The Burton Story Board Camber is a high-performance women's snowboard built for powerful turns, deep carves, and all-mountain versatility. Designed with a Directional Camber profile, it provides the precision and stability of camber underfoot while offering effortless float in powder with its slightly rockered nose. The stiff flex delivers aggressive response, making it perfect for riders who charge hard in all conditions. The Super Fly II™ 700G Core with Dualzone EGD™ enhances edge hold and energy transfer, while Carbon Highlights keep the board lightweight and lively. The Sintered WFO Base ensures maximum speed and durability, keeping you gliding through all snow conditions. Featuring a bold, artistic top sheet that blends modern graphics with a mountain-inspired aesthetic, the Story Board stands out in both style and performance. Whether carving groomers, dropping into steeps, or floating through fresh powder, this board is built for women who want to ride hard and tell their own story on the mountain.
