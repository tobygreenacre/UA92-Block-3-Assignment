    Bataleon Spirit

    £429.95

    The Bataleon Spirit is a confidence-boosting, all-mountain snowboard designed for beginner to intermediate riders looking for a smooth and forgiving ride. Featuring Bataleon’s Triple Base Technology (3BT), this board has uplifted contact points that make edge catches nearly impossible, providing effortless turns and a playful, catch-free feel. The Directional Twin Shape offers versatility, making it easy to ride both regular and switch, while the medium-soft flex ensures a comfortable and responsive experience for all conditions. Built with a Core Core of lightweight poplar and hardwood reinforcements, the Spirit provides durability without feeling heavy. The Hyper Glide S Base keeps it fast and low-maintenance, perfect for riders who just want to focus on having fun. With its stylish, minimalist design and clean color palette, the Spirit is an inviting and approachable board that helps riders progress with ease and confidence, whether cruising groomers, learning tricks, or venturing into fresh snow.
