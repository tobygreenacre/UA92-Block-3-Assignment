    Burton 3D Fish Directional Flat Top

    £630

    The Burton 3D Fish Directional Flat Top is a legendary powder snowboard designed for effortless float and a surf-like feel in deep snow. Featuring Burton’s 3D convex shaping, this board has a spoon-like nose that enhances lift and smooth transitions, making powder riding feel completely effortless. The Directional Flat Top profile provides a stable and forgiving ride while keeping the nose up and the tail responsive for quick turns. Its 30mm taper and setback stance ensure unmatched float and agility in deep snow, while the Sintered WFO Base keeps it fast in all conditions. The lightweight Super Fly II 700G Core enhances maneuverability without sacrificing strength, making it a go-to for powder purists. With its sleek, minimalist design and subtle fish-tail shape, the 3D Fish is built for riders who want to surf the mountain and make the most of every powder day.
