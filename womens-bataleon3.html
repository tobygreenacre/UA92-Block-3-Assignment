    Bataleon Feelbetter

    £459.95

    The Bataleon Feelbetter is an easygoing, progression-friendly snowboard designed to help riders build confidence and have fun across the entire mountain. Featuring Bataleon’s Triple Base Technology (3BT), it has uplifted contact points that make turn initiation effortless while reducing the chances of catching an edge, creating a smooth and forgiving ride. The Directional Twin Shape provides versatility, allowing for stable carving and easy switch riding, while the soft-medium flex makes it perfect for playful freestyle moves and all-mountain cruising. Built with a Light Core made from a mix of poplar and paulownia wood, the board stays lightweight and responsive without sacrificing durability. The Hyper Glide S Base ensures a fast and low-maintenance ride, ideal for both groomers and park laps. With its sleek, minimalist design and soft pastel color palette, the Feelbetter not only rides well but also looks great doing it. Whether you're learning new tricks, perfecting carves, or just looking for a fun, confidence-boosting board, the Feelbetter is built to make every run more enjoyable.
