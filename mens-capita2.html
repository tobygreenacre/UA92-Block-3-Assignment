    Capita SB Slush Slashers

    The Capita Spring Break Slush Slasher is the ultimate party board, designed for riders who want to have fun carving, slashing, and surfing the mountain. With a retro-inspired graphic and bold branding, the Slush Slasher embodies fun and creativity, making every ride feel like a surf session on snow.
