    Salomon Huck Knife

    £430

    The Salomon Huck Knife is a true twin freestyle powerhouse built for park riders and all-mountain tricksters who demand precision, pop, and control. Featuring a Quad Camber profile, it delivers explosive ollies, stable edge hold, and responsive handling, making it ideal for aggressive jumps, rails, and fast carving. The Popster Booster Core with carbon stringers enhances energy transfer for maximum snap, while the Sintered Base ensures top-tier speed and durability. The EQ Rad Sidecut provides a perfect blend of control and maneuverability, allowing for smooth transitions and quick edge changes. With a futuristic, neon-infused graphic and bold Salomon branding, the Huck Knife stands out both in the park and on the mountain. Whether stomping technical tricks, locking into rails, or charging kickers, this board is built for riders pushing the limits of freestyle progression.
