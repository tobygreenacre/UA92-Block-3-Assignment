    Ride Benchwarmer

    £510

    The Ride Benchwarmer is a high-performance freestyle snowboard built for aggressive park riders who demand explosive pop, precision, and stability. Featuring a Twin Extra Camber profile, this board maximizes edge control and energy transfer, making it perfect for high-speed carving, technical rail tricks, and sending big jumps. The Performance™ Core, constructed from a blend of aspen, bamboo, and paulownia, keeps the board lightweight yet strong, while Carbon Array 3™ provides enhanced response and stability on landings. Impact Plates™ and Slim Walls™ increase durability and absorb shock, ensuring a smooth ride even in rough terrain. The bold, futuristic graphic paired with its sleek, aggressive design makes the Benchwarmer stand out both in the park and across the mountain. Whether you're dialing in tricks or charging with confidence, this board is built for riders who refuse to sit on the sidelines.
