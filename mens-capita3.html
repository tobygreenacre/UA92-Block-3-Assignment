    Capita The Big Nice Burgundy

    £579.95

    The Capita Big Nice Burgundy is a limited-edition all-mountain freestyle snowboard designed for riders who want a playful yet powerful ride. With a true twin shape and medium flex, it excels in both park and all-mountain conditions, delivering the perfect balance of stability and maneuverability. The Resort V1 Profile blends camber underfoot with flat and rocker zones at the tips, offering explosive pop, solid edge control, and a forgiving feel for presses and butters. Built with the lightweight and durable Dual Blaster Ashpop Core, this board is engineered for riders who demand versatility and performance. The bold burgundy and yellow color scheme, combined with its fun, cartoon-inspired graphics, gives it a distinctive personality that stands out on the slopes. Whether you're hitting jumps, locking into rails, or carving up groomers, the Big Nice Burgundy delivers a smooth, responsive ride with a whole lot of style.
