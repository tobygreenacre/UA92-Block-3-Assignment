    Capita Paradise

    £479.95

    The Capita Paradise is a versatile all-mountain snowboard designed for riders who want a perfect balance of power, playfulness, and control. Built with a Resort V3 Profile, it combines camber underfoot for pop and edge hold with rocker at the tips for a smooth, catch-free ride, making it ideal for everything from carving groomers to floating in powder. The True Twin Shape allows for effortless switch riding, while the lightweight FSC® Certified Multizone Core provides durability and responsiveness without unnecessary weight. The Superdrive™ EX Base ensures speed and longevity, keeping you gliding effortlessly across all terrain. With its soft pink and purple nature-inspired graphic and bold Capita branding on the base, the Paradise offers both a stylish aesthetic and high-performance versatility. Whether you're sending jumps, cruising through trees, or just enjoying all-mountain freeriding, this board is built to handle it all with confidence and ease.
