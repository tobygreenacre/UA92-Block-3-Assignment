    Ride Zero x Trevor Andrew

    £465

    The Ride Zero x Trevor Andrew is a high-energy freestyle snowboard designed in collaboration with artist and snowboard legend Trevor Andrew, blending performance with a bold, rebellious aesthetic. Built with a true twin shape and a hybrid camber profile, it delivers the perfect mix of power, pop, and playfulness, making it ideal for both park riding and all-mountain freestyle exploration. The Performance™ Core, made from a blend of aspen, bamboo, and paulownia, ensures durability and responsiveness, while Carbon Array 3™ and Slim Walls™ provide enhanced control, impact absorption, and smooth energy transfer. The graphic, featuring Trevor Andrew’s signature graffiti-inspired artwork, gives the board a raw, street-style aesthetic that stands out on the mountain. Whether you're hitting jumps, locking into rails, or carving fast laps, the Ride Zero x Trevor Andrew is built for riders who want top-tier performance with an unmistakable artistic edge.
