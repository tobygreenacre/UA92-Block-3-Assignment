    Burton Blossom Camber

    £585

    The Burton Blossom Camber is a high-performance freestyle snowboard built for creative riders who want precision, power, and playfulness in the park and beyond. Featuring a traditional camber profile, it delivers unmatched pop, edge hold, and stability, making it ideal for stomping big jumps, locking into rails, and carving with confidence. The Twin Shape ensures perfect balance for switch riding, while the Super Fly II™ 700G Core with Dualzone EGD™ enhances response and durability. Triax™ Fiberglass adds torsional flex for improved control, and the Sintered WFO Base keeps the board fast and durable in all conditions. With a striking artistic top sheet and bold Burton branding, the Blossom Camber stands out as both a high-performance board and a work of art. Whether you're hitting the park, launching off side hits, or shredding all-mountain freestyle lines, the Blossom is designed to bring out your most creative riding.
