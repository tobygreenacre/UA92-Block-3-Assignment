    Bataleon Push Up

    £499.95

    The Bataleon Push Up is a versatile all-mountain snowboard designed for female riders who want a perfect blend of playfulness, power, and control. Featuring Triple Base Technology (3BT) with uplifted contact points, this board delivers a catch-free ride while maintaining stability and responsiveness for carving, jumps, and all-mountain freestyle fun. The medium flex provides a balance of pop and forgiveness, making it ideal for both progressing riders and experienced shredders. The Light Core, built with a blend of paulownia and poplar, ensures durability without added weight, while the Hyper Glide S Base keeps it fast and smooth in all conditions. With its sleek, modern design and elegant color palette, the Push Up stands out with style while offering a confident and dynamic ride. Whether you're cruising groomers, floating through powder, or hitting the park, this board is built to handle it all with ease.
