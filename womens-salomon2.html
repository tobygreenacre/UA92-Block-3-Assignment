    Salomon Bliss

    £350

    The Salomon Bliss is a women's all-mountain snowboard designed for riders looking for a balance of control and playfulness. A hybrid camber profile provides stability on hardpack while maintaining a forgiving feel for freestyle fun. The gradient white-to-black top sheet with subtle topographic details and a mountain landscape adds a serene aesthetic, while the minimalist black base with a pink sunburst logo keeps it sleek and stylish. Perfect for riders who want versatility with a smooth, effortless ride, the Bliss is built for exploration and progression.
